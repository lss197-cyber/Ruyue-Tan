About Ruyue

Based in Miami Florida Ruyue Tan is a pianist originally from Wuhan China. She is known for her energetic personality and incredible piano playing and teaching

One of her recent Recitals

Early life and Career

Born in China, Ruyue began formal musical training at the age of five. She was later admitted to the Middle School Affiliated with the China Conservatory of Music in Beijing, studying with Shuman Ren. In 2016, she continued her education at the Feliks Nowowiejski Academy of Music in Bydgoszcz, Poland, where she studied under Ewa Pobłocka. She then pursued her master’s degree in piano performance at Indiana University's Jacobs School of Music, studying with Edward Auer. Currently, Ruyue is completing her Doctor of Musical Arts degree at the Frost School of Music, University of Miami, where she studies with Kevin P. Kenner under a high scholarship and teaching assistantship.
